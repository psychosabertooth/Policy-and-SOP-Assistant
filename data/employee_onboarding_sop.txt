EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING PROCEDURE
TelecomCo – SOP Document
Document ID: SOP-HR-2025-003
Effective Date: February 1, 2025
Version: 2.5
Classification: Internal

1. PURPOSE
This SOP outlines the standard procedure for onboarding new employees at
TelecomCo. It ensures every new hire receives consistent access, training,
and orientation regardless of department or location.

2. SCOPE
Applies to all new hires including:
- Full-time employees
- Part-time employees
- Contractors (with modified steps as noted)
- Interns

3. PRE-ARRIVAL (HR – 5 Business Days Before Start Date)

Step 1: System Account Provisioning
- HR submits the New Hire Request Form in ServiceNow.
- IT creates accounts: Active Directory, email (firstname.lastname@telecomco.com),
  Microsoft 365, VPN access.
- Badge access provisioned for assigned building/floor.
- For contractors: Access limited to project-specific systems only.

Step 2: Equipment Preparation
- IT prepares laptop (Windows or Mac per role requirements).
- Standard software image includes: Windows 11 / macOS, Microsoft 365,
  Teams, VPN client, endpoint security agent.
- Headset and monitors allocated based on role (customer-facing roles get
  dual monitors + certified headset).
- Equipment shipped to remote employees 3 days before start date.

Step 3: Workspace Setup
- Facilities assigns desk/office space.
- Manager confirms team seating arrangement.
- Welcome packet placed at desk: ID badge, parking pass, company swag.

4. DAY ONE ORIENTATION

Step 4: HR Welcome Session (9:00 AM – 11:00 AM)
- Company overview and mission
- Benefits enrollment walkthrough (medical, dental, vision, 401k)
- Review of key policies:
  - Acceptable Use Policy
  - Customer Data Privacy Policy
  - Code of Conduct
  - Anti-Harassment Policy
- Sign acknowledgment forms in DocuSign

Step 5: IT Setup Session (11:00 AM – 12:00 PM)
- Laptop configuration verification
- Password creation and MFA enrollment (Microsoft Authenticator)
- VPN setup and test
- Overview of IT support: helpdesk@telecomco.com | ext. 5555

Step 6: Manager Introduction (1:00 PM – 3:00 PM)
- Meet the team
- Review 30/60/90 day plan
- Assign onboarding buddy (peer mentor for first 90 days)
- Tour of office / virtual tour for remote employees

Step 7: Systems Training (3:00 PM – 5:00 PM)
- Access verification for all role-specific systems
- Quick-start training for primary tools (CRM, ticketing, etc.)
- Schedule follow-up training sessions in LMS

5. FIRST WEEK (Days 2-5)

Step 8: Mandatory Training (via LMS)
- Information Security Awareness (2 hours)
- Data Privacy & CPNI Training (1.5 hours)
- Anti-Bribery and Ethics (1 hour)
- Workplace Safety (30 minutes)
- All training must be completed within first 7 days.

Step 9: Department-Specific Onboarding
- Each department has supplementary onboarding:
  - Network Operations: NOC tools training, shift schedule review
  - Customer Care: Call handling procedures, escalation matrix
  - Sales: Product catalog training, quoting system access
  - Engineering: Lab access, code repository access (GitHub Enterprise)

Step 10: HR Check-In
- HR schedules a 30-minute check-in at end of first week.
- Address any access issues, questions, or concerns.
- Confirm benefits enrollment is complete.

6. FIRST 30 DAYS

Step 11: Performance Goal Setting
- Manager and employee collaborate on 30/60/90 day goals.
- Goals documented in the Performance Management System (Workday).
- At least one goal must be training/certification related.

Step 12: Compliance Verification
- HR verifies all mandatory training is complete.
- Manager confirms system access is appropriate (no over-provisioning).
- Employee completes the Onboarding Feedback Survey.

7. 90-DAY REVIEW

Step 13: Probationary Review
- Manager conducts formal 90-day performance review.
- Review covers: job performance, cultural fit, training completion.
- Decision: Confirm employment, extend probation, or terminate.
- Results documented in Workday.

Step 14: Onboarding Closure
- HR closes the onboarding ticket in ServiceNow.
- Onboarding buddy assignment concludes.
- Employee added to regular performance review cycle.

8. OFFBOARDING NOTE
When an employee leaves TelecomCo:
- Manager submits Termination Request in ServiceNow.
- IT disables all accounts within 4 hours of departure.
- Badge access revoked same day.
- Equipment return coordinated by IT (remote employees: prepaid shipping label).
- Exit interview conducted by HR within 5 business days.
